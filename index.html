ABOUT US:

Make Sokoto Green is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to environmental protection, conservation, and fostering a cleaner, healthier society in Sokoto State, Nigeria. It aims to create a sustainable, environmentally conscious, and ecosystem-restored Sokoto State, promoting a greener and healthier environment for future generations.

RATIONALE FOR THE PROJECT

Make Sokoto Green envisions a thriving Sokoto State with a robust environment, empowered citizens, and a sustainable future. The project is designed to address the critical need for environmental restoration and community empowerment in Sokoto. Deforestation and environmental degradation are pressing issues in the region, leading to problems like soil erosion, desertification, and reduced biodiversity. 

MISSION

To significantly increase tree covers in Sokoto State, raise public awareness about environmental sustainability, and empower communities through nature-based solutions.
